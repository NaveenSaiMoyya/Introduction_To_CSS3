TRANSITIONS

-> when eleemnts transition from one state to another, we can alter their appearance.
eg:
   - when hovering over link, changing color.
   - when img comes into focus, changing size.


4 Transition Properties:

- transition-property 
- transition-duration 
- transition-timing-function
- transition-delay


Setting up transitions follows 3 steps:

1 - defining the element.
2 - choosing elements for transition.
3 - defining new values. (must combine these with pseudo-class)
